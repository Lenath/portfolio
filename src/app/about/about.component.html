About Me

As an IT specialist with a passion for coding and a keen interest in emerging technologies, such as VR/AR,
I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and management skills to the table.

I am committed to driving innovation in the digital world by creating solutions for the IIoT.
My academic background, diverse skill set, and professional experience have significantly enhanced my capabilities within the dynamic field of information technology.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Applied Informatics with a specialization in Information Systems, providing me with a strong foundation in software development and IT solutions. My thirst for knowledge led me to pursue a Master's degree in Management with a focus on Marketing and Logistics, where I honed my strategic thinking and leadership abilities. Furthermore, I have a Postgraduate degree in Business Administration (MBA), which has expanded my understanding of business dynamics and organizational management.

I began my professional journey as a Front-end Developer at ABB Corporate Research Center, where I contributed to an exciting IIoT project. Our mission was to create the groundbreaking IIoT platform, known as ABB Abilty, which aimed to transform the industrial landscape. Working in collaboration with talented teams from diverse countries, I gained invaluable insights into global perspectives on technology and innovation.

In 2022, I transitioned to the role of a Statistical Engineer at RBC Bearings, where my focus is primarily on data management and analytics. I specialize in the creation of data-driven dashboards, a pivotal component in the ongoing development of a comprehensive cloud solution for IIoT. Our collaboration with the Dodge Team is pivotal in making digital transformation a tangible reality by creating an IIoT Platform named Dodge OPTIFY, and I am excited to continue contributing to this dynamic field of innovation.

In addition to my professional experience, I am committed to staying at the forefront of technology.
I hold the Microsoft AZ-900 certification, demonstrating my expertise in Microsoft Azure and cloud computing solutions. This certification, along with my practical experience, allows me to create innovative, scalable, and secure solutions in the cloud environment.
